With the largest and longest-standing field conservation program in the world, WCS works with governments, Indigenous Peoples, and local communities in more than 56 countries to support the conservation of over 20 million square kilometers of the highest ecological integrity areas in Nature's Strongholds across the globe.

WCS works across the full spectrum of conservation, in preventing the loss of, and in protecting and restoring, nature. We do this because the solutions to the most imminent threats to our own survival — climate change, biodiversity collapse, and zoonotic pandemics — lie in intact nature.
