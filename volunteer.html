Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about volunteering with WCS

Do I need previous experience to volunteer?

While some positions require specific skills or experience, many of our volunteer opportunities are open to individuals with a passion for conservation and a willingness to learn. We provide comprehensive training for all volunteers.

Are there age restrictions for volunteers?

Most positions require volunteers to be at least 18 years old. However, we do offer some youth volunteer programs for teenagers aged 14-17 with parental consent.

Do you provide accommodation for international volunteers?

For long-term field positions in remote locations, we typically provide basic accommodation. For other international opportunities, volunteers are responsible for their own accommodation, though we can provide guidance and recommendations.

How much time do I need to commit?

Time commitments vary by position. Local zoo/aquarium roles might require 4-8 hours per week, while field research positions often require longer commitments of 3-6 months. We try to be flexible to accommodate different availability.
