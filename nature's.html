Nature's Strongholds

Protecting Earth's most irreplaceable places for wildlife and people.

Our Approach Our Initiatives

WCS Impact's vision for Nature's Strongholds is to safeguard the planet's most biologically important landscapes, those areas so critical that their conservation is essential for the survival of countless species and for maintaining the health of our global ecosystems.

These are places of exceptional biodiversity, where nature's processes still largely function intact, and where human communities live in harmony with the wild. We focus our efforts on these strongholds because they offer the greatest return on conservation investment, providing havens for species on the brink and vital services for humanity.

From the dense rainforests of the Amazon to the vast plains of the Serengeti, and from the coral reefs of the Pacific to the frigid poles, our strongholds represent the last great wild places on Earth.

Strongholds for Wildlife and Humanity

Our conservation efforts are concentrated in areas vital for species survival and ecosystem health. These strongholds represent the best chance for nature's long-term resilience.

Our Approach

At WCS Impact, our approach to protecting Nature's Strongholds is multifaceted, combining scientific research, community engagement, policy advocacy, and on-the-ground action. We work hand-in-hand with local communities, indigenous peoples, and governments to develop and implement sustainable conservation strategies that benefit both wildlife and people.

We believe that effective conservation must be locally led and globally supported. Our teams on the ground monitor biodiversity, combat illegal wildlife trade, restore degraded habitats, and promote sustainable livelihoods. Through these integrated efforts, we aim to ensure the long-term viability of these critical ecosystems.

Key Pillars of Our Work

Protecting Species

Focusing on endangered and endemic species within these strongholds, ensuring their populations are stable and thriving.

Safeguarding Habitats

Implementing strategies to prevent habitat loss and degradation, from deforestation to ocean pollution.

Empowering Communities

Working with local communities to develop sustainable practices that reduce reliance on unsustainable resource extraction.

Combating Illicit Trade

Disrupting wildlife trafficking networks and strengthening law enforcement.

Influencing Policy

Advocating for stronger environmental policies and protected area designations at national and international levels.

Restoration & Recovery

Rehabilitating degraded ecosystems and supporting the recovery of threatened species populations.
